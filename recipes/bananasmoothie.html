Banana Smoothie

Description:

Ingredients

  • One Frozen Banana
  • Peanut Butter
  • Protein Powder
  • 0% Greek Yogurt
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Grab a blender and set to the Smoothie setting
  2. Add in Frozen Banana, Peanut Butter, Protein Powder, and Greek Yogurt to blender
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. If your smoothie is too runny or if you would like it thicker, add in additonal ice until desired consistency is reached
  5. Pour into serving glass and enjoy!
