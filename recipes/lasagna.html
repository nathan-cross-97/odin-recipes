Lasagna

Description:

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sauce
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Grab an 8x8 Baking tray and layer a lasagna sheet on the bottom.
  2. Add a layer of pasta sauce on top of the lasgna sheet
  3. Add a generous layer of cheese on top of the pasta sauce
  4. Add another lasagna sheet on top of the sheet
  5. Repeat steps 2, 3, and 4 until your baking tray is filled to the top
  6. Add Salt and Pepper and put in the oven at 450F for 20 Minutes
  7. After the 20 minutes has passed, remove Lasagna and let chill for 5 minutes
  8. Cut into four squares and enjoy!
